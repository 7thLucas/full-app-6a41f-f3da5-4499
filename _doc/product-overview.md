# Product Overview — AI Resume Screening Platform

## Product Name
RecruitIQ

## Tagline
Screen smarter. Hire faster.

## What It Is
RecruitIQ is an AI-powered resume screening platform built for recruiters and hiring managers. It automates the evaluation of job applicants by comparing resumes against job descriptions, surfacing candidate strengths and weaknesses, identifying skill gaps, and ranking candidates by fit — so recruiters make faster, better-informed hiring decisions.

## Core Users / Personas

- **Recruiter**: Uploads job descriptions and candidate resumes, reviews AI-generated match scores and summaries, moves candidates through hiring pipeline stages.
- **Hiring Manager**: Reviews shortlisted candidates with AI summaries, approves or rejects recommendations, tracks pipeline progress.

## Problem Being Solved
Recruiters are overwhelmed by high application volumes. Manual resume review is slow, inconsistent, and subjective — leading to missed talent, poor shortlists, and delayed hiring decisions. Teams waste hours reading irrelevant resumes before finding a qualified candidate.

## Solution
RecruitIQ uses AI to instantly compare each resume against job requirements, producing:
- A match score (0–100%)
- A strengths summary
- A weaknesses summary
- A list of missing skills
- A hiring recommendation

This compresses hours of manual screening into minutes, with consistent, explainable results across every candidate.

## Core Features

1. **Resume & JD Upload** — Recruiters upload resumes (PDF/DOCX) and paste or upload job descriptions. Bulk upload supported.
2. **AI Candidate Analysis** — Per-candidate AI evaluation: match score, strengths, weaknesses, missing skills, and overall recommendation.
3. **Candidate Ranking** — Candidates sorted by fit score, with AI-recommended top picks highlighted.
4. **Hiring Pipeline** — Kanban-style pipeline with stages: Applied → Screened → Shortlisted → Interview → Offer → Hired / Rejected.
5. **Skill Gap Report** — Visual summary of skills most commonly missing across the applicant pool for a given job.
6. **Job Management** — Create and manage multiple open roles, each with its own applicant pool and pipeline.
7. **Recruiter Dashboard** — Overview of open roles, total applicants, screening progress, and shortlist counts.

## Brand & Tone
- **Voice**: Professional, confident, data-driven. Not robotic — clear and direct.
- **Brand colors**: Deep navy primary (`#1E3A5F`), electric blue accent (`#2D7DD2`), white backgrounds, light slate surfaces.
- **Typography**: Headings — Space Grotesk. Body — Inter.
- **Feel**: Enterprise-ready but approachable. Clean, minimal, high-trust.

## Strategic Principles
- AI output is always explainable — every score comes with a summary, never a black box.
- Recruiters stay in control — AI recommends, humans decide.
- Speed is the core value prop — screening that took hours now takes minutes.
- Pipeline discipline — every candidate has a clear status at every stage.

## Scope (MVP)
- Single organization workspace
- Upload resumes + JDs, get AI analysis
- Pipeline management per job
- Dashboard with screening metrics
- No integrations (ATS, email, calendar) in MVP

## Weekly Verified Operations (North Star)
**Primary operation**: Resume screening completed (AI analysis run on a candidate for a specific job)

Projection basis:
- Assumption: 3 recruiters active per week
- Assumption: Each processes 20 candidates/week
- = **60 resume screenings / week**

Value per screening: ~25 min saved (manual review time) × $35/hr recruiter cost = **~$14.58 saved per screening**

Weekly value: 60 × $14.58 = **~$875/week**
Annual value: $875 × 52 = **~$45,500/year**
