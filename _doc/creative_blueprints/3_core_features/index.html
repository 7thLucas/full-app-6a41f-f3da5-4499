Core Features

Everything your hiring team needs, built in

Recruiter Hiring Manager
📄

Resume & JD Upload

Bulk upload resumes (PDF/DOCX) and paste or upload job descriptions. One job, many candidates — instantly organized.

🤖

AI Candidate Analysis

Match score, strengths, weaknesses, missing skills, and hiring recommendation — per candidate, against the job description.

🏆

Candidate Ranking

All applicants sorted by AI fit score. Top-recommended candidates highlighted — the best rises automatically.

🗂️

Hiring Pipeline

Kanban pipeline: Applied → Screened → Shortlisted → Interview → Offer → Hired. Every candidate has a clear stage.

📊

Skill Gap Report

Visual breakdown of skills most commonly missing across the applicant pool — helps refine job requirements over time.

🖥️

Recruiter Dashboard

Open roles, total applicants, screening progress, and shortlist counts — at a glance, always current.
