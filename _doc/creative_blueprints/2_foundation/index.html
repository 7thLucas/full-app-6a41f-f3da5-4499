The Foundation

Why hiring is broken — and how we fix it

✕
The Problem

Volume overwhelm

Recruiters receive hundreds of applications per role — manually reading every resume is impractical and inconsistent.

Subjective shortlisting

Without a structured comparison, strong candidates get missed and weak ones advance — wasting interview slots and leadership time.

Slow time-to-hire

Days spent on initial screening delay offers, and top candidates accept competing offers before a decision is made.

✓
The Solution

AI match scoring

{{config.appName}} compares every resume against the job description in seconds, producing a 0–100% fit score with full explanation.

Explainable summaries

Every candidate gets a strengths, weaknesses, and missing-skills breakdown — no black box, recruiter stays in control.

Pipeline clarity

Candidates move from Applied → Screened → Shortlisted → Interview → Offer — giving the whole team a real-time view of hiring progress.
