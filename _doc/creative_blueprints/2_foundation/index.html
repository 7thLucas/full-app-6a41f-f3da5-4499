The Foundation

Why hiring is broken — and how we fix it

✕
The Problem

Volume overwhelm

Recruiters receive hundreds of applications per role. Manually reading every resume is impractical and unsustainable.

Inconsistent evaluations

Without a structured comparison, strong candidates get missed and weak ones advance — depending on who reviews them and when.

Slow time-to-hire

Days spent on initial screening delay offers — top candidates accept competing offers before a decision is made.

✓
The Solution

AI match scoring

{{config.appName}} compares every resume against the job description in seconds — 0–100% fit score with full explanation, every time.

Explainable summaries

Every candidate gets a strengths, weaknesses, and missing-skills breakdown. No black box — recruiter stays in control of every decision.

Pipeline clarity

Candidates move Applied → Screened → Shortlisted → Interview → Offer → Hired. Whole team sees real-time hiring progress.
